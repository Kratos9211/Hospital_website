Gastroenterology

Gastroenterology Department focused on the digestive system and its disorders. Diseases affecting the gastrointestinal tract, which include diseases of the esophagus, stomach, small intestine, colon and rectum, pancreas, gallbladder, bile ducts and liver

Urology & Urosurgery

Urology is a Surgical Specialities, covering the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the kidneys, ureters, bladder, prostate, female and male reproductive and urinary organs. Our service is based in a state-of-the-art Urology Centre at suyash Hospital.

Laparoscopic Surgery

Laparoscopy is an operation performed in the abdomen or pelvis, diagnosis or therapeutic interventions using small incisions with the aid of a camera. It can also be used to carry out surgical procedures, such as removing a damaged or diseased organ

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Department of Neurology at Suyash Hospitals provides best-in-class advanced neurological services. We providing specialized care in stroke, epilepsy, head and spinal injuries, brain tumors, neuropathic & neuromuscular disorder and movement disorders

Cardiology & Cardiac Cathlab

Cardiology and cathlab department deals with the disorders of the heart and circulatory system. it includes medical diagnosis and treatment of congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, heart failure, valvular heart disease and electrophysiology.

Nephrology & Kidney Dialysis

Nephrology is a specialty of medicine focused on the kidneys, specifically normal kidney function and kidney disease, the preservation of kidney health and the treatment of kidney disease. Dialysis and kidney transplant are both treatments for kidney failure.
